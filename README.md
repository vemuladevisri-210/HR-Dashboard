# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📖 Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze workforce data and provide actionable insights into employee demographics, attrition, recruitment performance, salary distribution, and organizational growth trends.

This dashboard helps HR teams and business stakeholders make data-driven decisions by visualizing key workforce metrics and identifying areas for improvement in employee retention and recruitment strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem Statement

Organizations often face challenges in understanding workforce trends, employee attrition, recruitment effectiveness, and departmental staffing patterns. Without proper analytics, HR teams may struggle to identify retention issues and optimize workforce planning.

This project aims to provide a centralized HR analytics solution that enables decision-makers to monitor workforce performance, evaluate hiring sources, and improve employee retention strategies.

## 📈 Key Insights

* Total Employees: **311**
* Female Employees: **176**
* Male Employees: **135**
* Attrition Rate: **33%**
* Average Salary: **69K**
* Average Service Years: **14.07 Years**
* Production Department has the highest employee concentration.
* Indeed, LinkedIn, and Google Search are among the most effective recruitment sources.
* The majority of employees belong to the **25–44 age group**, representing the core workforce.

---

## ✅ Final Conclusion

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of workforce performance and employee demographics. The analysis highlights a relatively high attrition rate, indicating opportunities to strengthen employee engagement and retention programs. Recruitment source analysis helps identify the most effective hiring channels, while workforce distribution insights support strategic HR planning.

By leveraging this dashboard, organizations can make informed decisions regarding talent acquisition, employee retention, workforce planning, and overall HR strategy.
